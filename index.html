The Gaps

Great Flight School. Untapped Growth Potential.

Rexair has an excellent reputation, multiple locations, and strong programs. But there are critical systems missing that are leaving revenue on the table.

No Student Progress Portal

Students have no way to see their flight hours, milestones, or checkride readiness online. Instructors and students lack a shared digital workspace, leading to miscommunication and dropout.

No Discovery Flight Self-Booking

You have great forms for program interest and campus tours, but discovery flights — the top-of-funnel conversion moment — still require a phone call. High-intent visitors want to book instantly.

No Student-Instructor Digital Hub

Lesson reminders, progress updates, training notes, milestone celebrations — there's no centralized place for ongoing student-instructor communication. Admin time adds up fast.

No Automated Review Collection

You have 15+ great testimonials on your site, but no systematic pipeline to collect new ones after every checkride. Consistent Google reviews drive rankings and trust.

Student Retention Risk

Without progress visibility and proactive re-engagement, students stall or drop off. No automated outreach when a student hasn't flown in weeks means lost revenue.

No Interactive Decision Tools

No cost calculator, no 529 eligibility checker, no program quiz. Prospects researching a $15k-$95k decision want to self-serve before picking up the phone.

With programs from PPL to ATP, each lost student = $15,000–$60,000+ in missed revenue

The right systems can close those gaps without adding headcount.
