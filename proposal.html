Proposed Services

1. International Student Landing Page + Funnel

Dedicated funnel for international prospects: M-1 visa process explained, US Dept of State recognition featured prominently, Purdue Global degree pathway, accelerated ATP timeline, FAA certification details. SEO-targeted for "FAA pilot training Florida international," "flight school M-1 visa USA," "foreign pilot training certificate USA."

Est. value: $4,000–$5,000

2. Cirrus Owner Marketing Hub

Dedicated Cirrus training pages: transition training, CSIP recurrency, CAPS training, Gen 6/7 aircraft specifics. Platinum status prominently featured. Targets "Cirrus training Naples FL," "Cirrus transition instructor Florida," "Cirrus SR20 SR22 training Southwest Florida." Cirrus owners are the highest-value training clients in general aviation.

Est. value: $3,500–$4,500

3. AI Lead Nurturing System

AI-powered interactive qualifier on the site: determines career vs. recreational, budget range, timeline, domestic vs. international, program interest. Routes to the right program page, collects email, and enters qualified leads into appropriate follow-up sequences. Answers common questions 24/7. The Purdue Global partnership means complex multi-touch sales — AI makes that scalable.

Est. value: $5,000–$7,000

4. Online Booking System

Cirrus intro flights, discovery flights, and accelerated program consultations bookable online. High-value clients — Cirrus owners, career-path pilots, international students — expect frictionless scheduling. Deposit collection, automated confirmations, and CRM integration.

Est. value: $2,500–$3,500

5. SEO Content Strategy

8 targeted posts covering: Cirrus training in Southwest Florida, accelerated ATP programs Florida, international pilot training USA M-1 visa, Purdue Global aviation degree path, 529 plans for pilot training, Naples vs. Sebring training, Rexair Accelerated Pilot Path breakdown. Each post targeting a distinct high-intent search audience.

Est. value: $3,000–$4,000

6. 529 Plan Content Expansion

Turn the existing 529 page into a full content strategy: FAQ page, blog post for parents researching aviation careers for their children, training cost calculator, and dedicated landing page. This differentiator is rare in the industry — let's own it as a brand position, not just an afterthought page.

Est. value: $2,000–$2,500
