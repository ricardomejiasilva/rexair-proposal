Proposed Services

Complete Digital Partnership

Total Value: $30,000+

A comprehensive suite of systems built specifically for Rexair's needs:

1. Student Progress Portal (Custom Web Application)

The core deliverable — a purpose-built platform for student and instructor communication.

  • Student Dashboard — Flight hours logged, milestones completed, progress toward checkride
  • Lesson Management — View upcoming lessons, past lesson notes, instructor feedback
  • Scheduling Integration — Book and manage lesson times within the portal
  • Training Materials Hub — Access study guides, pre-flight checklists, program documents
  • Messaging System — Direct student-instructor communication thread per student
  • Checkride Readiness — Visual indicators for requirements completion (hours, maneuvers, knowledge test)
  • Multi-Program Support — Works across PPL, Instrument, Commercial, Multi-Engine, ATP, and Cirrus programs
  • Multi-Location Support — Handles Naples and Sebring students in one system
  • Instructor Admin Panel — Manage all students, log hours, add notes, track fleet utilization
  • Management Dashboard — Bird's-eye view of all active students, program completion rates, revenue metrics

2. Conversion Tools & Discovery Flight Booking

You already capture leads well. These tools help convert more of them into enrolled students.

  • Discovery Flight Self-Booking — Instant online scheduling for Naples (and Sebring), so high-intent visitors can book without calling
  • Interactive Cost Calculator — Per-program cost estimator that helps prospects understand the investment before they reach out
  • "Is Flight Training Right for Me?" Quiz — Engaging tool that segments visitors by program interest and captures contact info
  • Optimized Program Landing Pages — Conversion-focused versions of your existing PPL, Instrument, Commercial, Cirrus, and Professional Pilot Path pages
  • Discovery Flight Follow-Up Automation — Automated sequence after discovery flights to convert attendees into enrolled students
  • Upsell Sequences — After PPL completion, automated nurture for Instrument Rating; after Instrument, nurture for Commercial — maximizing lifetime student value

3. Automation & Custom AI Tools

  • SMS Lesson Reminders — Automated text 24 hours before scheduled flights (reduces no-shows)
  • Weather-Aware Notifications — Alert students when conditions are good for makeup flights
  • Milestone Celebrations — Automated emails for first solo, written test pass, checkride completion
  • Stalled Student Re-engagement — Automated outreach when students haven't flown in 2+ weeks
  • Review Request Automation — Triggered after checkrides, with direct Google Reviews link
  • Student Referral Program — Tracking, automated reward notifications, referral leaderboard
  • Custom AI Solutions — Purpose-built AI tools tailored to your specific operational bottlenecks. Whether it's automating repetitive admin tasks, building an AI assistant that answers prospective student questions 24/7, or creating intelligent scheduling that accounts for weather, aircraft availability, and instructor workload — I can build custom solutions to problems unique to Rexair

4. SEO & Conversion Optimization

  • Local SEO — Optimization for Naples and Sebring markets ("flight school Naples FL", "learn to fly Sebring", etc.)
  • Google Business Profile — Optimization for both locations
  • Schema Markup — Flight school structured data for rich search results

  • Google Analytics 4 — Setup with custom events and goals

Enhance your existing 529 and Purdue Global content with interactive tools that convert browsing parents into inquiries.

  • Training Cost Calculator — Per-program cost estimator with financing breakdown
